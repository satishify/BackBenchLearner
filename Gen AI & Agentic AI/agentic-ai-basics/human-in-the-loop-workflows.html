Human-in-the-Loop Workflows

Human-in-the-loop (HITL) means people approve or correct AI decisions at critical points.

flowchart TD A[Agent drafts action] --> B{High risk?} B -- Yes --> C[Human review] C --> D[Approve or reject] B -- No --> E[Auto execute] D --> E

Where HITL is important

  • Financial transactions.
  • Legal/compliance outputs.
  • Security or account access changes.
  • External communication in sensitive cases.

Benefits

  • Reduces risk and improves trust.
  • Creates feedback loops for model improvement.
  • Supports governance and accountability.
