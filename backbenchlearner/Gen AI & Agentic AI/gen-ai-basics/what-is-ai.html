What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI is the broad field of building systems that perform tasks requiring intelligence, such as reasoning, decision making, language understanding, and problem solving.

Classic AI viewpoints

  • Think humanly
  • Think rationally
  • Act humanly
  • Act rationally

In modern products, the focus is often on acting rationally: producing the best possible outcome for a given goal.

AI timeline highlights

  • 1956: Dartmouth workshop (early AI formalization).
  • Symbolic AI era: logic, rule-based systems, expert systems.
  • AI winters: progress slowed due to scalability and data limits.
  • ML and deep learning revived AI with data-driven methods.

Real-world examples

  • Maps finding fastest routes.
  • Spam filtering and fraud detection.
  • Voice assistants and chatbots.
