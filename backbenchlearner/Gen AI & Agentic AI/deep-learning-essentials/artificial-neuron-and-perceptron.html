Artificial Neuron and Perceptron

An artificial neuron is a small mathematical unit inspired by biological neurons. A perceptron is one of the earliest neuron-based learning models.

Neuron computation

z = w1x1 + w2x2 + ... + wnxn + b, then y = f(z)

Here, w are weights, b is bias, and f is an activation function.

flowchart LR X1[x1] --> S[Weighted sum + bias] X2[x2] --> S X3[x3] --> S S --> A[Activation f(z)] A --> Y[Output y]

Perceptron limitation

A single perceptron can only solve linearly separable problems. This is why deep multi-layer models became important.
